Project Team Grad Group 2: Housing, COSI 116A F23

Zuhaeer Islam, Hongqian Li, Yixuan He, Minjie Tang

Project-long Course Project as part of COSI 116A: Information Visualization, taught by Prof. Dylan Cashman, Brandeis University.

Motivation

Our decision to center our class project on visualizing data regarding homelessness and rent inequality in New England stems from its immediate relevance to our community in Boston. As students in a city facing similar challenges, we recognize the importance of shedding light on these pressing issues to spark awareness and drive change.

By employing data visualization techniques, we aim to create impactful and accessible representations of this complex data, bridging the gap between statistics and societal understanding. Our goal is to not only inform but also evoke empathy, advocate for policy changes, and empower informed decision-making among stakeholders and policymakers. This project not only aligns with our academic pursuits but also underscores our commitment to addressing critical social issues in our region, fostering a more inclusive and empathetic community.
